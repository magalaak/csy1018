    What is the purpose?

    It appears that people don’t always get the right idea about what network engineers do. The idea that people get is that the engineers just sit down behind the desk and just monitor the network. The purpose of this website is to show what happens behind closed doors and most importantly, to make sure the audience learn and get more knowledge about this.
